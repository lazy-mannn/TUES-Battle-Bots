First connect the esp32 to the pc and run the esp32blemac program.
Then write the mac addresss in Sixaxis pairing tool. https://sixaxispairtool.en.lo4d.com/windows
After that flash the control program.
When you press the PS4 button the joystick should connect to the esp32. REMEMBER the ds4 can have the mac address of one device at a time.
